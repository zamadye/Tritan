# Polymarket Momentum Trading — Agent Knowledge Base

## How Polymarket Prices Work
- YES price = market's implied probability (0.01 = 1%, 0.99 = 99%)
- Price moves when new information changes trader sentiment
- Low-liquidity markets (<$5k) move easily on small news
- High-volume markets (>$50k/day) reflect strong consensus — harder to beat
- Price near 0.10-0.30: market thinks unlikely but not impossible → high upside if catalyst hits
- Price near 0.70-0.90: market thinks likely → bet NO if you see weakness

## What Moves Prices (Catalysts by Strength)

### STRONG catalysts (price moves 10-30% within hours):
- Official government announcement or policy decision
- Breaking news from primary source (Reuters, AP, official statement)
- Live event result (game score, vote count, price crossing threshold)
- Regulatory decision or court ruling
- Central bank announcement (Fed, ECB)

### MEDIUM catalysts (price moves 5-15% within 24h):
- Major media coverage shift (multiple outlets covering same angle)
- Social media volume spike (Twitter/X trending, Reddit front page)
- Analyst or expert prediction from credible source
- Related market moving significantly (BTC price affects BTC prediction markets)
- Odds movement on sports betting sites >5%

### WEAK catalysts (price moves 2-8%, uncertain):
- Single news article without confirmation
- Social sentiment shift without volume
- Indirect correlation (oil price affects Iran deal probability)
- Historical pattern without current trigger

## Geopolitical Markets — STRICT RULES
- ONLY bet if: official government statement, confirmed military action, or signed agreement
- "Air activity", "leaked roadmap", "chatter" = WEAK signal → SKIP
- Iran/ceasefire: need official announcement from US State Dept or Iranian govt
- High volume ($500k+) = market has information → follow price direction if confirmed news



### Crypto Markets
- BTC price prediction markets: watch actual BTC price vs target
- If BTC is within 3% of target with 2+ days left → strong YES signal
- Fear & Greed index <20 = extreme fear → BTC likely to bounce
- Options gamma squeeze near expiry (Friday) amplifies moves
- Volume spike on Binance/Coinbase = real momentum

### Geopolitical Markets
- Iran/US: watch official statements, not rumors
- Ceasefire markets: military activity reports are key signals
- Peace deal markets: diplomatic meeting announcements move price significantly
- These markets often stay flat for days then spike on news

### Sports Markets
- NBA/NFL: injury reports (official team injury report) are primary signal
- Tennis: live scores from ATP/WTA official site
- Series markets: momentum shifts after each game
- Avoid betting before game starts without injury/lineup info

### Politics Markets
- Arrest/indictment markets: DOJ/FBI official statements only
- Election markets: polling aggregates + early voting data
- Policy markets: Congressional vote schedules, committee hearings

## Entry Price Strategy
- 10-25%: High upside, small bet, needs strong catalyst
- 25-45%: Sweet spot for momentum — enough room to move both ways
- 45-55%: Near 50/50, need very strong directional catalyst
- 55-75%: Bet NO if you see weakness, YES if strong confirmation
- 75-90%: Only bet NO if you have concrete counter-evidence

## Exit Strategy (Current Settings)
- Take Profit: +30% return on position value → exit immediately
- Trailing Stop: 15% from peak → locks in profit on pullbacks
- Stop Loss: -25% return → cut losses fast
- Max Hold: 12 hours → don't hold overnight without strong thesis

## What NOT to Do
- Don't bet on "base rate" alone (no catalyst = no edge)
- Don't bet on markets resolving in <2 hours (too late)
- Don't bet on markets with spread >10% (illiquid)
- Don't average down on losing positions
- Don't bet >$1 per trade in high-frequency mode
- Don't bet on sports without live data or injury report

## Compounding Rules
- Current: flat $0.50-$1.00 per bet
- After 5 consecutive wins: bet size increases by 10%
- After 5 consecutive losses: bet size decreases by 10%
- Never bet more than 5% of bankroll on single trade
